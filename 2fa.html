Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Access your account settings by logging in to your profile.

Locate the security section and find the option for Two-Factor Authentication.

Click on the “Enable 2FA” button to start the setup process.

Download a trusted authenticator app like Google Authenticator or Authy.

Open the authenticator app and choose to add a new account.

Scan the QR code displayed on your account page using the app.

If scanning fails, manually enter the provided secret key into the app.

Enter the verification code generated by the authenticator app.

Ensure that the code you entered matches the one on your device.

Save the backup codes offered by the system in a secure location.

Complete the verification process to activate Two-Factor Authentication.

Test the 2FA setup by logging out and then logging back in.

When prompted, enter the 2FA code from your authenticator app.

If you lose access to your device, use a backup code to regain entry.

Keep your authenticator app updated for security improvements.

Verify that your device’s time settings are accurate for proper code generation.

Review your account security settings after enabling 2FA.

Remember to store your backup codes safely away from your device.

Regularly check for any notifications regarding your 2FA status.

Complete the process and enjoy the enhanced security of your account.
